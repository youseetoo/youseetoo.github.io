Design your microscope

Our drag-and-drop configurator lets you design custom optical setups from UC2 cube modules. Preview beam paths, check compatibility, and export a complete build package.

How it works

Three steps from idea to instrument

1. Choose components

Browse the cube library. Drag lenses, mirrors, LEDs, cameras, and filter cubes onto the grid.

2. Configure & preview

Adjust parameters, see the resulting beam path, and verify alignment. The tool warns you about conflicts.

3. Export & build

Export a bill of materials, STL files for 3D printing, and a step-by-step assembly guide.
