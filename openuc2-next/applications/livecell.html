Live-Cell & Time-Lapse

Long-term imaging with incubator compatibility

Monitor living cells over hours or days with openUC2. The compact form factor allows placement inside standard CO2 incubators. ESP32-based WiFi control means no cables through the incubator door. Automated focus maintenance and multi-position acquisition enable unattended experiments.

Get in touchSee FRAME

Technical specifications

EnvironmentCO2 incubator compatible (fits inside standard incubator)
ControlWiFi (ESP32), no cable feedthrough required
Imaging modesPhase contrast, DPC, brightfield, fluorescence
Time resolutionConfigurable intervals (seconds to hours)
Multi-positionUp to 96 positions per experiment
Focus maintenanceSoftware autofocus per time point

Key capabilities

Incubator-ready

Small footprint fits inside standard CO2 incubators. WiFi control — no cables through the door.

Multi-position acquisition

Define up to 96 positions and image them in sequence at each time point.

Phase contrast & DPC

LED matrix illumination enables label-free phase contrast and differential phase contrast imaging.

Automated focus

Software autofocus compensates for thermal drift and maintains sharpness over days-long experiments.

On the roadmap

AI-based cell tracking integration
Environmental sensor logging (T, CO2, humidity)
Integration with liquid handling robots
