Histology & Slide Scanning

Key capabilities

🔬 Full slide coverage

130×90 mm XY travel covers 75×25 mm standard slides with room to spare. Multi-slide holders available for batch scanning.

🧩 Automated tile & stitch

ImSwitch handles multi-tile acquisition, autofocus per tile, and stitching into seamless panoramic images. Export as TIFF, OME-ZARR, or other standard formats.

🎨 Brightfield & beyond

Combine with fluorescence modules for IF/IHC staining. Quantitative phase imaging with DPC for label-free contrast on unstained sections.
