Live-Cell & Time-Lapse Imaging

Why FRAME for live-cell imaging

🌡️ Incubator compatible

FRAME's open stage design has ample room above the sample for stage-top incubators (e.g. Okolab, ibidi). Maintain 37°C, 5% CO₂, and humidity throughout your experiment.

⏱️ Automated time-lapse

Script multi-position, multi-channel time-lapse experiments via Python SDK or ImSwitch GUI. Auto-focus at each timepoint keeps your cells sharp.

📡 Remote monitoring

Browser-based control means you can check your experiment from anywhere on your network. Get alerts, adjust parameters, or add positions mid-run.
