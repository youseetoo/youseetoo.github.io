How the Configurator works

1️⃣

Choose your components

Browse the part library: lenses, mirrors, LEDs, lasers, cameras, filters, beamsplitters. Each component is a cube module with defined optical properties.

2️⃣

Place on the grid

Drag cubes onto a 2D grid that represents your baseplate. Arrange them to form your desired beam path — the tool shows you the optical layout in real time.

3️⃣

Export & build

Export a bill of materials, assembly instructions, and 3D-printable STL files. Or share your design with the community and get feedback.
